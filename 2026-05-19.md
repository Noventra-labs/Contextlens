## 🧠 Improvements & Learnings

📌 **New Rules / Discoveries**
* Utilizing VS Code's `context.secrets` (SecretStorage backed by OS keychain/credential manager) is an excellent architectural pattern for caching sensitive tokens locally, preventing unnecessary backend reads and reducing Firestore DB load.

🔄 **Optimizations & Reflections**
* When introducing encryption-at-rest to an existing database, prefixing ciphertext (e.g. `enc:v1:`) enables graceful fallback/passthrough for legacy unencrypted records during the migration window.
